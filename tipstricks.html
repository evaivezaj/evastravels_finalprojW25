Tips & Tricks

Traveling can be a hastle, but there are ways to reduce the stress and fully enjoy your trip! Here is what I reccomend:

Tips

  • Pack light! You don't need to bring your whole closet with you
  • Learn a few phrases in the local language
  • Try the local food. You won't regret it!
  • Be open to new experiences
  • Keep an open mind and be flexible with your plans
  • Do your RESEARCH!

Tricks

  • Use a packing list to make sure you don't forget anything
  • Use a travel app to keep track of your itinerary
  • Use a VPN when using public Wi-Fi
  • Use a travel credit card to earn points and save money
  • Use a travel pillow for long flights or car rides
  • Use a portable charger to keep your devices charged on the go
  • Take all your cash out while still in the US!! The withdraw rates are a lot lower here.
  • Double check with your bank if you need to report your travel so they don't think your card is lost
