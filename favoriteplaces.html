My Favorite Places

Montenegro

Specifics: Cem, Ulqin, Kotor

Montenegro is a small country located in the Balkans. It is known for its mountains, beaches, and beautiful coastal cities that have an ancient feel. I have a little bias with this location as it is where my family lives. However, it's beauty is undeniable. My favorite places to visit here are Cem (a river between mountains), Ulqin, and Kotor! The photos are presented in that order.

Albania

AKA- The Motherland

Albania is the motherland for me so of course I love it! It's very beautiful with vast nature scenes and coastal cities. My favorite place here is 'Green Coast' which is a small coastal beach town located in the south. Other highlights include Ksamil and Vlore -which are both very beautiful beaches with large cities surrounding it.

Italy

Specifics: Rome, Vatican City

Italy is a country that is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and delicious food. My favorite places are Rome and Vatican City. Within these I love the Trevi Fountain, the Panthenon, and St. Peters Basillica.
